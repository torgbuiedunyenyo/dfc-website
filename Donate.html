dream farm commons is a generative resource providing opportunities for artists and audiences to engage their curiosity and foster community through culture and creativity. Beyond functioning as a gallery dream farm commons is a home to artist crits, film screenings, book clubs, dinners, talks, dance parties, sonic mediations and poetry readings.
