with Kaethe Weingarten Ph.D.

Kaethe Weingarten Ph.D., founder of the Witness to Witness Program talks about their work helping people who work at the border.

We are honored to have Kaethe Weingarten Ph.D., the founder of the Witness to Witness Program, to talk about the important work that the program is doing to help the helpers who are working in the current humanitarian disaster created by this administration in relation to immigration. The W2W Program has 38 volunteers meeting with front line workers across the country to support them in the challenging work they are doing with migrants, detainees and asylum seekers, many of whom are unaccompanied children.

We welcome you to join us to learn more about the program and how you can help the helpers.
