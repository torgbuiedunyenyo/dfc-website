A discourse, conversation and performance with Ignacio Valera and Beatriz Escobar

We are excited to pair Ignacio Valero, California College of the Arts professor and amazing futuristic thinker, with visual and performance artist Beatriz Escobar and see what they come up with! As they have both been traveling this is what we know: Ignacio has recently been thinking and writing about the Emotariat, the colonization of our emotions and new imaginaries. Beatriz has been contemplating decolonizing the horizon, as an invitation to rethink modern paradigms of linearity and predictability. There will be talk, there will be performance and there will be visuality.
