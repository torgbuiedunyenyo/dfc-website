Food Monument
November 21, 6-8PM

As part of Rad Tender exhibition DFC joined in the National Action of Fall of Freedom-- artists all over the country doing actions to decry the madness and assaults to democracy, immigrants and food safety. We built a pyramid of canned goods that were brought to Dream Farm Commons on Friday and shared this food with our 15th St Neighbors, who have been facing food insecurity, all deeply appreciated.

We additionally raised over $1000 100% contributed directly to a local foodbank that has been deeply involved in supporting service workers over the last 50+ years.
