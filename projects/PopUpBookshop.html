For one week only, we are presenting an extraordinary collection of images and photography books curated by 3SSSTUDIOS.COM at Dream Farm Commons. This is a collection of infrequently found art books from international and local publishers in China, Italy, Britain, and the Bay Area framed by new works of Shisi Huang, Yong Xiao, Nanxi Zhou. The works are gathered under a wide but very specific umbrella of twists of truth and representation: the verifiable occurrence made strange, the supernatural as captured or fabricated, decontextualized imagery, questionable documentation, broken frames, the fantastic and the ordinary juxtaposed. The fine art books are presented with new works by three emerging artists whose own work engages with tenderly unstable truth.
